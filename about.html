What is Toastology?

Hey there! We're excited to present you with our first project, a personality test inspired by the theory formulated by Isabel Myers and Katharine Briggs. This test is designed to assess your preferences across four dimensions of personality types, providing insights into your strengths, weaknesses, and tendencies. By answering the 12 questions, you will discover which personality type best describes you, represented by a unique toast character.
